We are committed

Our organization is deeply committed to the preservation of wild horses, recognizing their vital role in our ecosystems and cultural heritage. We take a hands-on approach to conservation, engaging in various initiatives that include habitat restoration, public education, and direct care for at-risk herds. Our dedicated team of volunteers and experts works tirelessly in the field, conducting regular assessments of wild horse populations and their environments. We collaborate with local communities and government agencies to implement sustainable practices that protect these magnificent animals while balancing the needs of surrounding ecosystems. Our outreach programs aim to raise awareness about the importance of wild horse preservation, providing resources and opportunities for people to get involved. By fostering a sense of stewardship and responsibility toward these majestic creatures, we hope to inspire future generations to cherish and protect the natural world. Through our hands-on efforts, we strive not only to safeguard wild horse populations but also to promote a harmonious coexistence between humans and wildlife.

Our Sponsors

Our organization is incredibly fortunate to have the support of a diverse group of upstanding sponsors who share our passion for preserving wild horses and promoting environmental sustainability. These sponsors, ranging from local businesses to national corporations, play a crucial role in funding our initiatives and amplifying our message. Their commitment goes beyond financial contributions; they actively engage in our events, volunteer their time, and lend their expertise to help advance our mission. Many of our sponsors have deep-rooted ties to the equine community and understand the importance of protecting these magnificent animals for future generations. Through collaborative partnerships, we’re able to expand our reach and enhance our programs, allowing us to implement innovative strategies for habitat restoration, community outreach, and education. Their generosity enables us to conduct essential research and develop resources that benefit both wild horses and the ecosystems they inhabit. Together, we are building a movement that celebrates the beauty and significance of wild horses, fostering a culture of stewardship that resonates far beyond our immediate efforts. We are immensely grateful for their unwavering support, as it empowers us to continue our vital work in conservation and advocacy.

Highly qualified

Wild Wild is a dedicated horse preservation organization committed to safeguarding the well-being of equines in our care while promoting the importance of responsible conservation. Our team comprises a diverse group of highly qualified professionals, including veterinarians, equine behaviorists, and experienced trainers, all of whom share a deep passion for horses and a wealth of knowledge in animal welfare. With years of hands-on experience, our staff excels in rehabilitation, education, and advocacy, ensuring that every horse receives the individualized attention it deserves. We engage with the community through workshops, outreach programs, and partnerships with local organizations, fostering a culture of respect and responsibility toward these magnificent animals. Our commitment to preserving horse heritage extends beyond immediate care; we actively participate in research and conservation efforts to protect endangered breeds and promote sustainable practices in equine management. At Wild Wild, we believe that through education and compassion, we can inspire future generations to cherish and protect these incredible creatures, making a lasting impact on the equine world.
